Hi, I’m Reuben — the creator of Make Social Media Boring. I designed this extension to help with my own addictive behaviour. After trying many productivity apps, I always felt like they did too much or not enough. This isn’t meant to lock you out of your favourite websites — it just makes those experiences less engaging. Your donations really help me improve this project and build more tools to help others.
